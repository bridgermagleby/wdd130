Whitewater Rafting Co.

White Water Rafting Co. is dedicated to creating thrilling outdoor experiences while ensuring safety and excellence. With expert guides leading breathtaking river journeys, we inspire a love for adventure, teamwork, and a deep connection with nature.

History

Founded in 2005 by adventure enthusiasts Sarah and Tom Rivers, White Water Rafting Co. started as a small outfit guiding rafting trips along the rushing waters of the Snake River.

Today, they offer everything from half-day excursions to multi-day whitewater expeditions, earning a reputation for thrilling adventures and exceptional customer service.
