White Water Rafting

Our mission is to provide thrilling and safe adventures on the water.

History

Founded in 2005 by adventure enthusiasts Sarah and Tom Rivers, White Water Rafting Co. started as a small outfit guiding rafting trips along the rushing waters of the Snake River. With a commitment to safety and unforgettable experiences, the company grew into one of the premier rafting tour operators in the Pacific Northwest. Today, they offer everything from half-day excursions to multi-day whitewater expeditions, earning a reputation for thrilling adventures and exceptional customer service.
